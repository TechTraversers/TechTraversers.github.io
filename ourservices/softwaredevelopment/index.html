  • Simple Terminal App (No graphics): $3
  • Simple Graphical App: $9

What application do these apps usually apply to?

Many times the app you want has already been made and we will switch you over to our IT Help Plan but in the cases that there is not an app for it, we will make one. Typically these apps apply to in-system processes like automating certain things, speedup your work, or even playing a simple game. We typically do not do apps like 'track my steps and calculate calories spent'. If you want an app of that type, your prices can (and probably will) go anywhere from $50 to $200

What quality can I expect from the app?

Considering the complexity of apps, you can expect your app to be medium to low grade quality, this is because we do NOT maintain the app unless you ask (and pay) us to do updates, add certain features, or we mess up something. This typically doesn't affect your apps' effectiveness, it just means that it has minimal features and will likely need updated every time your operating system changes (like going from Windows 10 to Windows 11)

When can I expect my program to be finished?

The amount of time to complete a program really depends on how complicated it is. While a simple text-only calculator app may take a couple of days, something like a graphical task organizer can take weeks to months. We're not full-time developers, but we'll do our best to be prompt.
